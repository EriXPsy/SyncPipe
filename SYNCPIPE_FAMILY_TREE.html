A theoretical lineage decides what to measure. A methodological lineage decides how to test it. Together they shaped the architecture, the descriptor set, and the audited evidence chain.

Theoretical lineage — what to measure

Coordination dynamics & metastability

Kelso; Haken — synchrony is a dynamic, time-varying process, not one static "level". Motivates measuring beyond the mean: structure, timing, distribution shape.

→ go beyond mean synchrony
↓

Interpersonal synchrony in psychology

Bernieri, Reznick & Rosenthal — dyadic coordination and the genuine-vs-pseudo distinction; Ramseyer & Tschacher establish windowed cross-correlation (WCC) of movement as the substrate.

→ WCC substrate + pseudo-pair logic
↓

Episode / state view of coupling

Synchrony comes and goes in episodes → threshold-state structure (dwell time, switching rate) and occupancy become first-class descriptors.

→ dwell, switching, occupancy
↓

Descriptor set & operationalisation

Intensity: mean_synchrony (ref), peak_amplitude · Structure: dwell_time, switching_rate · Shape: bimodality, entropy · Timing: first_peak_time, inter_peak_cv · Occupancy: fraction_above.

Methodological lineage — how to test it

Surrogate-data hypothesis testing

Theiler et al. (surrogate method); Schreiber & Schmitz (IAAFT, preserve spectrum + amplitude distribution); Lykken & Venables (percentile-based standardised cut-offs). → signal-level IAAFT existence null + per-dyad surrogate threshold.

→ existence null + threshold
↓

Block bootstrap for dependent series

Künsch (moving block); Politis & Romano (circular block, wrap-around tail handling); Ramseyer & Tschacher segment shuffling. → cyclic block-bootstrap peak-timing null (sound but underpowered → deferred to v2).

→ block-bootstrap null
↓

Design controls for confounds

Pseudo-pair (partner identity), time-shift (alignment), across-stimulus shuffle (shared input). → the design-control audit, step 2 of the chain.

→ design-control audit
↓

Ground truth + incremental value

Kuramoto / parametric ground truth (PGT-2/3, EGT-4/5) for known-answer recovery; nested logistic-regression incremental AUC on real data (Lerique 2024) to show information beyond intensity.

→ GT batteries + incremental AUC

The three-step audited evidence chain

The spine everything hangs on. Order matters — do not skip or reorder.

1
null: signal-level IAAFT

Synchrony-existence audit

Do WCC descriptors exceed independent autocorrelated-signal nulls? Necessary but not sufficient — does not rule out shared stimulus or co-presence.

2
pseudo-pair · time-shift · stim-shuffle

Design-control audit

Is the effect partner-specific and time-locked, or an artifact of shared input and mere co-presence?

3
dyad-paired permutation · BH-FDR (m=3)

Group condition inference

Do audited descriptors differ across conditions? FDR over {peak_amplitude, dwell_time, switching_rate}; mean_synchrony is reference only.

What backs the descriptors

Validation evidence — and one capability honestly deferred.

ground truth

Known-answer recovery

PGT-2 (structure), PGT-3 (temporal), EGT-4 (emergent), GT-5 (Gordon-conditions) batteries recover the intended descriptors on simulations with a known answer.

real data

Incremental AUC

On Lerique 2024 (rest vs task), timing descriptors add cross-validated AUC over a mean_synchrony baseline — information beyond intensity, not magnitude proxies.

deferred to v2

Peak-timing existence null

The circular time-shift null was falsified; the cyclic block-bootstrap null is methodologically sound (no over-rejection on a structureless quasi-null) but underpowered on single-peak morphologies and confounded on trace-level real data. The timing descriptors stay exploratory; a signal-level existence test is v2 work.

Two grounded thresholds

Cut-offs derived from a null distribution, not arbitrary r-value anchors.

Per-dyad surrogate threshold

95th percentile of that dyad's own IAAFT-surrogate WCC values — "the level this dyad would reach by chance". Use for within-dyad existence.

Session- / condition-pooled threshold

One threshold pooled across all dyads (or per condition). Use for between-dyad comparability of episode features (dwell, switching), so "episode" means the same thing for everyone.
